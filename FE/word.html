explore

KK: /ɪkˈsplɔr/ 釋義: 探索

例句: The best way to explore the countryside is on foot.

衍生詞: exploration, explorer

同義詞: investigate, look into

字根: ex- (向外), -plor (哭叫)

解釋: The word 'explore' is derived from the Latin word 'explorare', meaning 'to cry out', which was later used metaphorically to refer to 'looking for information or searching for something'.
